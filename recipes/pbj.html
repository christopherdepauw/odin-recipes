Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

This American classic is sure to impress both friend and foe at your next dinner party. A simple yet elegant masterpiece consisting of three essential ingredients. Without one, this dish cannot exist!

Ingredients

  • Jelly (of your choice)
  • Peanut Butter (of your choice)
  • Two slices of bread (of your choice)

Steps

  1. Set two slices of your favorite bread flat on a surface of your choosing (I prefer a cutting board, but go crazy!)
  2. Smear peanut butter on one side of one piece of bread
  3. Now here's the secret: Smear the jelly on top of the smeared peanut butter not on the other piece of bread (you'll thank me later!)
  4. Enclose the sandwich with the second, smearless piece of bread
  5. Enjoy!
