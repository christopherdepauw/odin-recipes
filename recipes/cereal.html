Cereal

Description

It's literally just whatever cereal you bought from the store and then you pour milk in it. Why are you here? Like and also subscribe, though.

Ingredients

  • Any cereal. Fruit loops. Cap'n crunch. Build your own. Doesn't matter!
  • Milk. Again, lots of flexibility here. One percent, two percent. Both are fine. Skim is for psychopaths.

Steps

Okay, here's where it gets complicated. Lol, jk. Simply,
  1. Grab a bowl and a spoon
  2. Fill them both with some cereal
  3. Top that cereal with some milk
  4. Eat it with the aforementioned spoon
  5. Voila!
