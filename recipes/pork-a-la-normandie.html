Pork a la Normandie

Description

A beautiful French stew with Pork and apples. This classic dish is the perfect easy dinner whether you're on a budget or on-the-go. A classic of French cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 900 grams pork neck
  • 3 pcs celeriac
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 5 sage leaves
  • 1 tbsp wheat flour
  • 5 dl apple cider
  • 3 dl whipped cream
  • 800g small potatoes
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp neutral oil
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • salt and pepper
    • After the dish has been in the oven
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp calvados
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped parsley
  • 2 apples cut into small cubes
    •

    Steps

    1. Cut pork into 1 inch cubes and season
    2. Sear pork until a nice crust is formed. 2-3 minutes. Move pork to plate
    3. In the same pan, sautee herb and vegetables until onions are transluscent
    4. Add cream, potatoes, and pork to pan
    5. Move pan to oven and bake at 200 celsius until pork reaches 67 celsius
    6. Top with fresh parsley, butter, dijon, calvados, and apple. Serve hot
